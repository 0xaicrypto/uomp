# Starknet Grants — Quick Note

## Relevant programs

1. **Seed Grants** — early-stage, non-dilutive funding for go-to-market.
   - https://www.starknet.io/grants/seed-grants/
   - Good fit for UOMP: open-source protocol, early traction, community feedback.

2. **Growth Grants** — later-stage teams with a working product in market.
   - https://www.starknet.io/grants/growth-grants/
   - Fit after a Starknet integration demo and initial partnerships.

3. **AI Portal / Talk to Us**
   - https://forms.reform.app/starkware/talk-to-us-ai-portal-starknet/3miubw
   - Use this to get in front of the Starknet AI team directly.

## Suggested grant narrative

- **Problem**: AI Agents require personal context, but current solutions force users to give up data sovereignty.
- **Solution**: UOMP provides a standard for user-owned memory with scoped, revocable, auditable access.
- **Starknet angle**: Leverage Starknet’s AA, cheap anchoring, and Cairo proofs to make the authorization layer verifiable and decentralized.
- **Deliverables**: Starknet-native example, registry adapter, documentation, community workshops.
- **Impact**: Better privacy defaults for all Starknet AI Agents; a reusable public good.

## Recommended next step

Fill out the AI Portal “Talk to Us” form first to validate fit, then prepare a Seed Grant application with a clear Starknet integration milestone.
